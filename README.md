# HackAttack3.0Project

Our idea is a Startup and Investor Ecosystem that bridges the gap between academia, founders, mentors, and investors. It is built around 4 pillars - A diagnostic questionnaire to start, a user profile and talent hub, resource database and an AI recommendation engine. Startups begin by completing a multi-step diagnostic that analyzes their stage, sector and key growth barriers (e.g. Do you have the necessary licenses and certifications?). Using this information, the platform generates a personalized AI-powered Startup Roadmap - a step-by-step guide that tells founders exactly what to do next, from validating their market and building an MVP to applying for grants, recruiting talent, and preparing for investors. Each step unlocks tailored recommendations, mentors, funding opportunities, and educational resources, turning the platform into a practical execution guide rather than just an information hub.

Beyond the roadmap, the ecosystem provides the tools and connections startups need to move forward. The resource databases (answer engine) offers verified explanation of complex topics such as regulatory compliance, grants, and operational requirements in clear, actionable language. A talent and community hub enables startups to recruit team members, connect with mentors and investors, and collaborate through secure messaging. Additional features like an Academia Masterclass Hub, an Interactive Question Builder, and a guided onboarding tour ensure founders can access expert knowledge, ask better questions, and navigate the ecosystem easily. Together, these components create an integrated platform where startups can diagnose their challenges, receive AI-guided roadmaps, access critical resources, and connect with the right people to accelerate their growth.
